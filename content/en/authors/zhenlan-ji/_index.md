## Profile

<p>I am an assistant professor at Nara Institute of Science and Technology (NAIST), Japan. Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree, advised by Prof. Shuai Wang, at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). In 2021, I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Nanjing University, Nanjing, China. My research interests include <strong>Software Engineering</strong> and <strong>Deep Learning</strong>, with a focus on <strong>Causality</strong> and <strong>LLM-based Agents</strong>. </p>
